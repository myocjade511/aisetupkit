📊 Pricing Strategy

New agency owners: Start at the low end of the range above. Raise prices every 3 clients.

Experienced: Start at the high end. Bundle setup + 3 months retainer for a discounted package.

Premium tier: Once you have 5+ case studies, double your prices. The market will pay it.

📦 Packaging Tips

• Always offer 3 tiers (Basic / Pro / Premium) — most people pick the middle one

• Bundle setup + retainer for higher perceived value

• Add a "Done For You" premium tier at 2x the Pro price

• Never discount — add value instead (more services, faster delivery)
