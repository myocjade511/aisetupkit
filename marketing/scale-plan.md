## Next Levers to Pull (Priority Order)

1. **Stripe checkout** — Wire real payment processing (need API key) — unlocks actual sales
2. **Email automation** — Connect AgentMail or ConvertKit to deliver kit + send sequences
3. **Affiliate program** — 20% commission on $97 = $19/sale — low cost, high reach
4. **Coaching upsell** — $497/mo for 4-week coaching (already built into product architecture)
5. **Agency white-label** — $997/setup for agencies to resell under their own brand
6. **Content engine** — Daily TikTok/Reels clips from the VSL + testimonials
7. **Lead magnet** — Free "5 AI Services You Can Sell Today" PDF → email capture → sequence
8. **Testimonials** — First 5 customers get free coaching in exchange for video testimonial

## Scale Checklist

- [ ] Stripe connected → checkout live
- [ ] Email delivery automated (purchase → access link delivered)
- [ ] Ad account pixel firing correctly
- [ ] Retargeting audience built (150+ visitors)
- [ ] First 3 customer testimonials collected
- [ ] Google Analytics (or alternative) tracking setup
- [ ] Affiliate program launched (5+ affiliates)

## Exit Criteria (When to Stop / Pivot)

- CPA > $30 after 2 weeks of testing → switch audience or creative
- CVR < 1% after 500 visitors → rewrite sales page
- Refund rate > 10% → improve deliverable quality or clarify offer
- ROAS < 2x after $2,000 spend → pause ads, go organic only
