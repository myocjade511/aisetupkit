Quick Answers: Starting Your AI Agency

Straightforward answers to the most common questions about building an AI automation business.

What is an AI automation agency?

An AI automation agency helps businesses automate repetitive tasks using artificial intelligence tools. Services include chatbot setup, content automation, voice agents, data analysis, and process automation. Clients pay monthly retainers for ongoing support.

How do I start an AI agency with no experience?

Use a done-for-you starter kit like AISetupKit that provides pre-built service menus, client scripts, and delivery systems. Pick one service, customize the template, and start pitching. No technical experience required — just the willingness to follow the system.

How much money can an AI agency make?

AI agency owners typically charge $500-$3,000 for setup projects and $300-$1,500/month for retainers. With 5-10 retainer clients, monthly revenue of $5,000-$10,000 is achievable within 3-6 months.

What AI services are in highest demand?

The top 5 AI services in demand are: AI chatbot setup, content automation, social media management, voice agents, and business process automation. These services solve immediate pain points for businesses of all sizes.
